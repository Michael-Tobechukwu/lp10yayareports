Who We Are

RCCG LP10 YAYA is the umbrella body of all youths and young adults in The Redeemed Christian Church of God Lagos Province 10, Agege, Lagos.

As a Christian, youth-focused body, our mission is to help youths and young adults fulfil their God-given potentials, while leading a life that pleases God.

Our provincial headquarters is at 2, Somoye street, Omotoye estate, Mulero, Agege, Lagos.
Our current Provincial Youth Pastor is Pastor Tope Omojola, who doubles as the Regional Youth Pastor of Region 37 YAYA.
